<p><p>Automobile Anti-Collision beam market includes three main types - Front Anti-collision Beam, Rear Anti-collision Beam, and Side Door Anti-collision Beam. Front Anti-collision Beams are installed in the front of the vehicle to absorb impact during a collision. Rear Anti-collision beams serve a similar purpose but at the rear end of the vehicle. Side Door Anti-collision beams are designed to protect the vehicle's side doors from damage in the event of a collision. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing the safety of the vehicle and its occupants.</p></p>
