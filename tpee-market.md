<p><p>TPEE (thermoplastic polyester elastomer) is available in three main market types: Modified grade, Flame Retardant grade, and High Performance grade. Modified grade TPEE is tailored for specific applications to enhance properties such as flexibility, toughness, or chemical resistance. Flame Retardant grade TPEE contains additives to inhibit combustion, making it suitable for applications where fire safety is a concern. High Performance grade TPEE is engineered to deliver superior performance characteristics, such as higher strength and durability, making it ideal for demanding applications.</p></p>
