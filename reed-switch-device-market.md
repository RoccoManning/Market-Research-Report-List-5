<p><p>Reed switches are electromagnetic devices that consist of two ferromagnetic contacts sealed in a glass envelope. They come in various types to suit different applications. Form A reed switches are normally open when there is no magnetic field present. Form B switches are normally closed in the absence of a magnetic field. Form C switches have both normally open and normally closed contacts. Other market types may include single-pole, double-throw switches or single-pole, single-throw switches with different contact configurations.</p></p>
