<p><p>Dynamic weighing systems are used to measure the weight of materials while they are in motion. There are two main types of dynamic weighing systems: mechanical and digital. Mechanical systems are traditional and use mechanical components like levers and springs for measurement. Digital systems, on the other hand, use electronic sensors and technology for more precise and efficient weighing. Both types are widely used in industries such as food processing, agriculture, and manufacturing for accurate weighing of materials on conveyor belts or in motion.</p></p>
