<p><p>Retarders are used in vehicles to slow them down without wearing out the traditional friction brakes. Electric retarders use electromagnetic induction to generate resistance and slow down the vehicle. Hydraulic retarders use fluid to create resistance and slow down the vehicle. Both types of retarders are effective in providing additional braking power and reducing wear on the traditional brake system. They are commonly used in heavy-duty vehicles such as trucks and buses to improve safety and control while driving downhill or carrying heavy loads.</p></p>
