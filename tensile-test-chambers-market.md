<p><p>Tensile test chambers are used to measure the strength and elasticity of materials. There are two main types of these chambers: single column type and dual column type. </p><p>Single column type chambers have a single column for applying tensile force to the material being tested. They are usually smaller and more compact, making them suitable for smaller testing applications.</p><p>Dual column type chambers have two columns that provide more stability and uniformity in applying force to the material. They are typically larger and more powerful, making them suitable for larger and more precise testing needs.</p></p>
