<p><p>Residential Earth Leakage Protection (ELP) markets are categorized based on the number of poles they have. 1P ELPs are single pole devices used for protecting single-phase circuits. 2P ELPs have two poles and are suitable for protecting split-phase or two-phase circuits. 3P ELPs are three pole devices designed for three-phase systems. There are also other specialized ELPs available for specific applications. Each type offers varying degrees of protection against electric shocks and can be chosen based on the specific requirements of the residential electrical system.</p></p>
