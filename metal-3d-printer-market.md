<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Metal 3D Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Selective Laser Melting (SLM)</li><li>Electronic Beam Melting (EBM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal 3D printers are categorized into two main types: Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electronic Beam Melting (EBM). SLM works by using a high-powered laser to selectively melt and fuse metal powder together layer by layer, resulting in accurate and complex metal parts. On the other hand, EBM uses an electron beam to melt and weld metal powder in a vacuum environment, allowing for the production of high-quality, fully dense metal parts with excellent mechanical properties.</p></p>
