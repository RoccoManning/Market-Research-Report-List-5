<p><p>Industrial area scan cameras are used for high-speed image capture in industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and pharmaceuticals. There are two main types of technology used in these cameras: CMOS and CCD. CMOS cameras are known for their low power consumption and high integration capabilities, making them ideal for compact applications. On the other hand, CCD cameras offer higher image quality and sensitivity, making them suitable for applications where image sharpness and clarity are crucial. Both types play a significant role in the industrial imaging market.</p></p>
