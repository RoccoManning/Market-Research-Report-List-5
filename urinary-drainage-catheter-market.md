<p><p>Urinary drainage catheters are medical devices used to manage urine flow in patients who are unable to empty their bladder naturally. These catheters come in three main types: rubber, plastic (PVC), and silicone. Rubber catheters are flexible and comfortable but can cause allergies in some patients. Plastic catheters, made from PVC, are durable and easy to insert. Silicone catheters are soft, flexible, and less likely to cause irritation. Each type has its own advantages and suitability for different patients' needs.</p></p>
