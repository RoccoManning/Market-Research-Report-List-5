<p><p>Plastic corrugated pipes are commonly available in two types: single wall corrugated and double wall corrugated. Single wall corrugated pipes are lightweight and flexible, making them ideal for drainage and ventilation systems. On the other hand, double wall corrugated pipes have an outer smooth wall and an inner corrugated wall, providing extra strength and durability. They are commonly used for underground utility installations and storm water management. Both types of pipes offer various diameters and lengths to suit different applications in the construction and agriculture industries.</p></p>
