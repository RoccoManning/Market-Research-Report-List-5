<p><ul><li>Rubber Sleeve</li><li>Magnetic Strip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Refrigerator door seal gaskets come in two main types: rubber sleeve and magnetic strip. Rubber sleeve gaskets are made of flexible rubber material that compresses when the door is closed, creating an airtight seal. Magnetic strip gaskets, on the other hand, use magnets embedded within the strip to create a secure seal when the door is closed. Both types of gaskets are designed to keep cold air in and warm air out, helping to maintain the temperature inside the refrigerator.</p></p>
