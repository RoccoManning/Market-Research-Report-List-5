<p><p>Railway traction motors are an essential component of locomotives and trains, providing the necessary power to propel them forward. There are two main types of traction motors - AC traction motors and DC traction motors. AC traction motors are known for their efficiency and ability to operate at variable speeds, making them popular in modern locomotives. On the other hand, DC traction motors are simpler in design and typically used in older locomotives. Both types play a crucial role in the efficient operation of railway systems.</p></p>
