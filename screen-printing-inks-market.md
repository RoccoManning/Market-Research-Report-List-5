<p><ul><li>Solvent-based Printing Inks</li><li>Water-based Printing Inks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Screen printing inks can be categorized into two main types: solvent-based printing inks and water-based printing inks. Solvent-based inks are known for their durability, resistance to fading, and ability to adhere to a wide range of substrates. They are commonly used in outdoor signage, automotive graphics, and industrial applications. On the other hand, water-based inks are environmentally-friendly, easy to clean up, and have low volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions. They are commonly used in textiles, paper, and cardboard printing.</p></p>
