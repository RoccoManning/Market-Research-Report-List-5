<p><p>A tapping sleeve and valve is a mechanical device used for making a branch connection on a pipe without interrupting the flow of the fluid inside the mainline. It consists of a sleeve that wraps around the mainline pipe, with a valve attached to it for controlling the flow to the branch line. Tapping sleeves and valves are commonly used in industries such as water and wastewater, oil and gas, and chemical processing.</p><p>The tapping sleeve and valve market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.8% during the forecast period.
