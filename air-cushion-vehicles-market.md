<p><p>Air-cushion vehicles, also known as hovercrafts, are divided into two main types based on their design features: skirted air-cushion vehicles and sidewall air-cushion vehicles. Skirted air-cushion vehicles are characterized by a flexible skirt that surrounds the base of the craft to contain the air cushion and create lift. On the other hand, sidewall air-cushion vehicles have solid sidewalls that act as barriers to keep the air cushion in place. Both types of air-cushion vehicles have their own unique advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
