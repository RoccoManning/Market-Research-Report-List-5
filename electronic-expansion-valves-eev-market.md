<p><strong>The Electronic Expansion Valves (EEV) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic EEVs</li><li>Electric EEVs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic Expansion Valves (EEVs) are commonly divided into two types: Electromagnetic EEVs and Electric EEVs. Electromagnetic EEVs utilize a solenoid coil to control the flow of refrigerant, providing accurate and precise regulation of the system. Electric EEVs, on the other hand, use a stepper motor to adjust the valve opening, offering greater flexibility and adaptability in various applications. Both types of EEVs are widely utilized in refrigeration and air conditioning systems for improved efficiency, performance, and energy savings.</p></p>
