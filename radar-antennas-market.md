<p><strong>The Radar Antennas Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Half Wave Dipole</li><li>Quarter Wave Dipole</li><li>Uniform Linear Array Antenna</li><li>Non-Uniform Array Antenna</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radar antennas can be classified into different types based on their design and configuration. Half Wave Dipole and Quarter Wave Dipole antennas are simple and commonly used for radar applications. Uniform Linear Array Antennas consist of multiple antenna elements arranged in a straight line, offering better beamforming capabilities. Non-Uniform Array Antennas have varying spacing between antenna elements, allowing for more flexibility in beam steering and shaping. Each type of radar antenna offers unique advantages and is suited for specific applications in radar systems.</p></p>
