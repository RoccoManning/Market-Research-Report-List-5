<p><p>Hydrophobic interaction chromatography resin is available in three main market types: natural polymer, synthetic polymer, and inorganic media. Natural polymer resins are derived from sources like agarose or cellulose, offering high biocompatibility. Synthetic polymer resins, such as polystyrene or polyacrylamide, provide customizable surface properties for specific applications. Inorganic media resins, like silica or alumina, offer high chemical stability and durability. Each type of resin offers unique advantages and suitability for different chromatography applications.</p></p>
