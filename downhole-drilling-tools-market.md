<p><p>Downhole drilling tools market consists of Fixed Cutter Drill Bit and Roller Cone Drill Bit segments. Fixed Cutter Drill Bits have fixed cutting elements like diamonds or tungsten carbide, providing high durability and improved performance in hard formations. Roller Cone Drill Bits have rotating cones with teeth that crush rock formations during drilling, suitable for softer formations. Both types cater to different drilling conditions and play a crucial role in the oil and gas industry for efficient and successful drilling operations.</p></p>
