AOI is used to inspect for defects on PCBs, while SPI checks the quality of solder paste deposits. AXI is used for inspecting hidden solder joints and components. These types of inspection equipment are crucial in ensuring the quality, reliability, and functionality of electronic assemblies in the surface mount technology (SMT) industry.</p></p>
