<p><p>Waterproof surgical tapes are available in two main types: PE (Polyethylene) and Non-Woven Fabric. PE tapes are made of a plastic material that provides high strength and adhesion, making them suitable for secure fixation of dressings and medical devices. Non-Woven Fabric tapes are made of a soft and breathable material that is gentle on the skin, making them ideal for sensitive areas or for patients with allergies. Both types of tapes are waterproof, ensuring they stay in place even in wet conditions.</p></p>
