<p><p>Linear Sorting Systems are designed for high-speed sorting of products in a straight line. They are commonly used in industries with high volume and continuous production needs. On the other hand, Loop Sorting Systems are more versatile and efficient as they allow products to circulate in a loop, providing more flexibility in the sorting process. They are commonly used in industries where products need to be sorted and rerouted multiple times for various processes. Both systems offer advanced automation and technology to optimize sorting efficiency.</p></p>
