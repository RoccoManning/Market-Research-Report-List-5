<p><p>Apron conveyors are mechanical systems used for transporting heavy materials such as ore, coal, rocks, and other bulk materials in various industries including mining, construction, and agriculture. They consist of a series of overlapping metal plates that are attached to a steel chain and move along a continuous loop. The apron conveyor is designed to withstand heavy loads and harsh operating conditions, making it a reliable solution for bulk material handling.</p><p>The global apron conveyor market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
