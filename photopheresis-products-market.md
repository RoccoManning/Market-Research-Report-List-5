<p><p>Photopheresis products market can be categorized into open system and closed system markets. In open system, the patient's blood is exposed to UV light outside the body before being returned, whereas in closed system, the whole procedure takes place inside a closed system. Open systems are more cost-effective and easier to maintain, while closed systems offer better control over the procedure. Both systems have their own advantages and disadvantages, catering to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
