<p><p>Closed cooling towers are commonly found in both industrial and commercial markets. Commercial closed cooling towers are often seen in office buildings, hotels, and data centers to regulate the temperature of HVAC systems. Both types of closed cooling towers work by circulating water through a closed loop system, where heat is dissipated through a heat exchanger before being recirculated.</p></p>
