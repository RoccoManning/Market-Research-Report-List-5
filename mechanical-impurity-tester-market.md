<p><p>Mechanical impurity testers are used to measure the amount of solid particles in a liquid sample. There are two common market types for these testers - Weight Method and Membrane Filtration. The Weight Method involves weighing the particles collected on a filter paper, while Membrane Filtration uses a filter membrane to trap particles for analysis. Both methods are effective in determining the level of mechanical impurities in a liquid sample and are widely used in industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and environmental testing.</p></p>
