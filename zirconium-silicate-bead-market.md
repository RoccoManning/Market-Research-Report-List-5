<p><p>Zirconium Silicate beads are categorized into two main types: sintered zirconium silicate and fused zirconium silicate. Sintered zirconium silicate beads are manufactured through a high temperature sintering process, resulting in a durable and long-lasting product. On the other hand, fused zirconium silicate beads are produced by melting zircon sand and other materials together, creating a strong and uniform structure. Both types of beads are widely used in the ceramics, electronics, and chemical industries for grinding, dispersing, and polishing applications.</p></p>
