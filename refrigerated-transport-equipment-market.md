<p><p>Refrigerated transport equipment includes various types of cooling systems designed to maintain specific temperatures during the transportation of perishable goods. Two common types in this market include air-blown evaporators and eutectic devices. Air-blown evaporators use fans to circulate cold air within a storage area, while eutectic devices utilize phase change materials to maintain consistent temperatures. Both types are essential for preserving the quality and freshness of food, pharmaceuticals, and other temperature-sensitive products during transit.</p></p>
