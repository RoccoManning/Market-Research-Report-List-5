<p><p>Specialty fuel additives are chemicals added to fuels to improve their performance and efficiency. Deposit control additives help prevent the build-up of deposits in engines, while cetane improvers increase the cetane number in diesel fuels for easier ignition. Antioxidants help prevent fuel oxidation, lubricity improvers reduce friction and wear in engines. Other market types include anti-corrosion additives, cold flow improvers, and bio-based additives. Each type of specialty fuel additive serves a specific purpose in enhancing fuel quality and engine performance.</p></p>
