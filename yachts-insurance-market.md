<p><ul><li>Actual Cash Value</li><li>Agreed Amount Value</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Yachts insurance market typically offers two types of coverage options: Actual Cash Value (ACV) and Agreed Amount Value (AAV). ACV policies provide coverage for the current market value of the yacht at the time of the claim, taking into account depreciation. On the other hand, AAV policies involve a pre-agreed upon amount that will be paid out in the event of a claim, regardless of the yacht's actual market value. Both options have their own benefits and drawbacks, depending on the individual needs of the yacht owner.</p></p>
