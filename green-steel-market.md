EAF involves melting scrap steel in an electric arc furnace, while MOE utilizes a process of electrolysis to extract oxygen from iron oxide. Both methods result in reduced carbon emissions and a more environmentally friendly steel production process, making them popular choices in the growing green steel market.</p></p>
