<p><p>Universal Testing Machine (UTM) market comprises of two main types: Electromechanical UTM and Hydraulic UTM. Electromechanical UTM uses an electric motor to generate force and is suitable for testing materials with lower force requirements. On the other hand, Hydraulic UTM uses hydraulic technology to generate force and is more suitable for high-force testing applications. Both types of UTM offer accurate and reliable testing capabilities for a wide range of materials and are commonly used in various industries such as construction, manufacturing, and research laboratories.</p></p>
