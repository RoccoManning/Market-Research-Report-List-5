<p><p>Aircraft exhaust systems come in two main types: Short Stack System and Collector System. Short Stack Systems are designed with a short exhaust pipe that helps reduce weight and improve engine performance in small aircraft. On the other hand, Collector Systems are commonly used in larger aircraft for their ability to collect exhaust gases from multiple cylinders into a single outlet, reducing noise and improving overall engine performance. Both systems play a crucial role in regulating emissions and enhancing the efficiency of aircraft engines.</p></p>
