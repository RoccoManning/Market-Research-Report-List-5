<p><p>Cold Rolled Electrical Steel Market consists of two main types: Oriented Electrical Steel and Non-oriented Electrical Steel. Oriented Electrical Steel is specifically designed to enhance magnetic properties in a specific direction, making it ideal for use in transformers and electric motors. On the other hand, Non-oriented Electrical Steel does not exhibit preferred magnetic orientation, making it suitable for applications such as electric generators and rotating machinery. Both types play a crucial role in various electrical applications due to their unique magnetic properties.</p></p>
