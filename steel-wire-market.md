<p><p>Steel wire market can be categorized into three main types based on the composition of the wire: Carbon steel wire, Alloy steel wire, and Stainless steel wire. Carbon steel wire is the most common type known for its high tensile strength and durability. Alloy steel wire is made by adding elements like manganese, nickel, and chromium to improve specific properties. Stainless steel wire is corrosion-resistant and ideal for applications requiring high resistance to heat and chemicals. Each type serves different purposes in various industries.</p></p>
