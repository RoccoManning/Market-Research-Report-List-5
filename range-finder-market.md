<p><p>Range finders are devices used to measure distance accurately. There are two main types in the market: laser range finders and ultrasonic range finders. Laser range finders emit a laser beam to determine the distance to an object, making them ideal for outdoor use and long distances. Ultrasonic range finders use sound waves to measure distance to objects, making them suitable for indoor measurements or environments with obstacles. Both types offer precise distance measurements, but each has specific applications based on their technology and capabilities.</p></p>
