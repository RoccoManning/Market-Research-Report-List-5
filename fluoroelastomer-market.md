<p><p>Fluoroelastomers refer to a type of synthetic rubber that is highly resistant to chemicals, heat, and oil. There are three main types within the Fluoroelastomer market - Fluorocarbon Elastomers, Fluorosilicone Elastomers, and Perfluoroelastomers. Fluorocarbon Elastomers are known for their excellent fuel and oil resistance, while Fluorosilicone Elastomers offer improved flexibility and low-temperature performance. Perfluoroelastomers are considered the highest performing type, with exceptional resistance to extreme temperatures and harsh chemicals, making them ideal for industries requiring superior durability and reliability.</p></p>
