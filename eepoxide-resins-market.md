<p><p>Epoxy resins are categorized into different types based on their physical properties and chemical composition. Halogenated epoxy resins are modified with halogen atoms for enhanced fire resistance. Multifunctional epoxy resins have multiple reactive groups for superior performance. Other epoxy resins include specialty formulations for specific applications such as electrical insulation. Each type of epoxy resin offers unique characteristics for various industrial needs.</p></p>
