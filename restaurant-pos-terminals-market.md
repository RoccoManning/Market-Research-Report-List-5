<p><p>Restaurant POS Terminals are divided into two main market types: Fixed POS Terminals and Mobile POS Terminals. Fixed POS Terminals are stationary systems found at the cashier counter, allowing servers to enter orders and process payments. On the other hand, Mobile POS Terminals are handheld devices used by servers to take orders and process payments directly at the table. Both types offer conveniences and efficiencies in restaurant operations, but Mobile POS Terminals provide the added flexibility of tableside service.</p></p>
