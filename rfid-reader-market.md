<p><p>RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) Readers are categorized into four types based on their frequency range: LF (Low Frequency), HF (High Frequency), UHF (Ultra High Frequency), and MW (Microwave) Readers. LF Readers operate at a frequency range of 125-134 kHz, suitable for short-range applications. HF Readers operate at 13.56 MHz and are commonly used in contactless payment systems. UHF Readers operate at 860-960 MHz and are ideal for long-range applications. MW Readers operate at frequencies above 1 GHz, providing high-speed communication and data transfer.</p></p>
