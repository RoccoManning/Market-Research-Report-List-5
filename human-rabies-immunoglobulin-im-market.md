<p><strong>The Human Rabies Immunoglobulin (IM) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ERIG</li><li>HRIG</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Human Rabies Immunoglobulin (IM) is available in two market types - Equine Rabies Immunoglobulin (ERIG) and Human Rabies Immunoglobulin (HRIG). ERIG is derived from horses and may cause allergic reactions in some individuals. HRIG, on the other hand, is derived from human plasma and is considered safer and more effective. Both types are administered along with the rabies vaccine to provide immediate protection against the virus. The choice between ERIG and HRIG depends on availability, cost, and individual patient factors.</p></p>
