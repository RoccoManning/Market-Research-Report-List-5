<p><ul><li>Automatic NDT Scanner</li><li>Semi-automatic NDT Scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>NDT scanners are categorized into two main types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic NDT scanners are fully automated systems that require minimal human intervention and are capable of performing inspections quickly and efficiently. They are often used in high-volume manufacturing environments. On the other hand, semi-automatic NDT scanners require some degree of manual control and intervention from operators. They offer more flexibility and customization options for inspections but may be slower than automatic scanners. Both types play crucial roles in various industries for non-destructive testing applications.</p></p>
