<p><p>Supraglottic airway devices are medical devices used to maintain an open airway during various medical procedures or in emergency situations. The main types of supraglottic airway devices include laryngeal mask airways, oropharyngeal airways, nasopharyngeal airways, and other supraglottic devices. Laryngeal mask airways are inserted into the pharynx to provide a clear airway passage, while oropharyngeal and nasopharyngeal airways are inserted into the mouth or nose respectively. Other supraglottic devices may include devices such as laryngeal tubes or esophageal-tracheal Combitube.</p></p>
