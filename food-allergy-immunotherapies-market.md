<p><strong>The Food Allergy Immunotherapies Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Subcutaneous Immunotherapy</li><li>Sublingual Immunotherapy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Food allergy immunotherapies refer to treatments that aim to desensitize the immune system to specific food allergens. Subcutaneous immunotherapy involves injecting small amounts of the allergen under the skin, gradually increasing the dosage over time. On the other hand, sublingual immunotherapy involves placing allergen extracts under the tongue. Both types of immunotherapy help the immune system build tolerance to the allergen, reducing the risk of severe allergic reactions. These treatments can greatly improve the quality of life for individuals with food allergies.</p></p>
