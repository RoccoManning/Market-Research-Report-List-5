<p><p>LVDT transducers are classified into two main types - AC Type and DC Type transducers. AC Type transducers are commonly used for dynamic measurements due to their high sensitivity and noise immunity. They are suitable for applications where continuous monitoring and precise measurements are required. On the other hand, DC Type transducers are preferred for static measurements as they offer higher accuracy and stability. They are commonly used in applications where precise positioning and displacement measurements are critical. These two types cater to different market needs based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
