<p><strong>The Converter Transformer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopolar</li><li>Back-To-Back</li><li>Bipolar</li><li>Multi-Terminal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Converter transformer market can be classified into four types based on their connection configuration: Monopolar, Back-To-Back, Bipolar, and Multi-Terminal. Monopolar systems use one pole conductor for power transmission, Back-To-Back systems connect two converters directly, Bipolar systems use two conductor poles between converters, and Multi-Terminal systems connect multiple converters in a single network. Each type offers different advantages and is chosen based on specific power transmission requirements and constraints in the market.</p></p>
