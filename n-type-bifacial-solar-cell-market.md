HJT cells offer high efficiency and low temperature coefficient. TOPCon cells feature a passivating oxide layer for reduced recombination and increased efficiency. Other technologies in the market include PERC and passivated emitter rear contact (PERC) cells, which offer alternative approaches to achieving high efficiency and bifacial capability in solar cells.</p></p>
