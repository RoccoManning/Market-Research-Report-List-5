They are suitable for most routine laboratory applications. On the other hand, PMMA cuvettes are known for their chemical resistance and durability. They are ideal for applications that require higher levels of chemical resistance, such as certain types of spectroscopic analysis. Both types of cuvettes are widely used in laboratories for various testing and analysis purposes.</p></p>
