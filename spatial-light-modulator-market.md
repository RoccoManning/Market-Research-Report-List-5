<p><p>Spatial Light Modulators (SLMs) are innovative devices used in a wide range of applications, including holography, microscopy, and telecommunications. Reflective SLMs function by reflecting light off a surface to modulate the intensity or phase of the light beam. Transmissive SLMs, on the other hand, modify the light passing through them by controlling the phase or intensity. Both types of SLMs offer unique advantages and are tailored to specific applications, making them essential components in the optics industry.</p></p>
