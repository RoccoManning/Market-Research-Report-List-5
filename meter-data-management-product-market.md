Cloud-based MDM products are hosted on a vendor's servers and accessed through the internet, providing flexibility and scalability. On-premises MDM products are installed and maintained on the utility's own servers, offering more control and security but requiring higher upfront costs. Both types of MDM products have their own advantages and considerations for utilities to evaluate.</p></p>
