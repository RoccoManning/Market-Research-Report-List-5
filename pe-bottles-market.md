<p><p>PE bottles are classified into three main market types based on the type of polyethylene used: HDPE, LDPE, and MDPE. HDPE bottles are strong, rigid, and commonly used for packaging liquids such as milk and household chemicals. LDPE bottles are flexible, lightweight, and used for squeeze bottles and plastic bags. MDPE bottles are a middle ground between HDPE and LDPE, offering a balance of strength and flexibility. Each type of PE bottle has specific properties and applications in the packaging industry.</p></p>
