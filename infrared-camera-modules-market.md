<p><p>Infrared camera modules are categorized into three types based on the wavelength they detect: shortwave, medium wave, and long wave. Shortwave infrared cameras detect wavelengths between 0.9 to 1.7 microns, ideal for industrial applications. Medium wave cameras detect wavelengths between 3 to 5 microns, commonly used in thermography and moisture detection. Long wave cameras detect wavelengths between 8 to 14 microns, suitable for thermal imaging and surveillance. Each type has specific strengths and applications in various industries.</p></p>
