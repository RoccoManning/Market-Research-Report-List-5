<p><p>Well stimulation materials market includes various types such as proppants, base fluid materials, polymer gelling agent, biocides and surfactants. Proppants are used to keep fractures open and improve well production. Base fluid materials are used as carriers for other chemicals. Polymer gelling agents help improve fluid viscosity for better proppant transport. Biocides are used to control bacterial growth, while surfactants help reduce surface tension and improve fluid performance. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing well stimulation and production efficiency.</p></p>
