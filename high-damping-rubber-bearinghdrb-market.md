<p><p>High Damping Rubber Bearings (HDRB) are seismic isolation devices that are used to reduce the impact of earthquakes on structures by absorbing and dissipating energy through the deformation of rubber material. Additionally, stringent building codes and regulations in seismic-prone areas are encouraging the adoption of HDRBs in new construction projects.</p><p>Key trends in the HDRB market include advancements in material technology to enhance the damping capacity and durability of rubber bearings, as well as the development of innovative designs to suit different structural applications.
