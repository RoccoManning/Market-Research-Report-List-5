<p><strong>The Industrial Resin 3D Printer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LFS Technology</li><li>FDM Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Resin 3D Printer Market consists of two types of technologies: LFS Technology and FDM Technology. LFS Technology, also known as Low Force Stereolithography, uses a flexible resin tank and a flexible build plate to create highly detailed and smooth prints. On the other hand, FDM Technology, or Fused Deposition Modeling, builds objects layer by layer by extruding molten thermoplastic material. Both technologies have their unique strengths and are used in various industrial applications for rapid prototyping and manufacturing processes.</p></p>
