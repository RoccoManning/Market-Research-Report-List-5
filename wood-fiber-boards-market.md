<p><ul><li>Porous Wood Fiber Board</li><li>Hard Wood Fiber Board</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wood fiber boards can be categorized into two main types based on their structural composition: porous wood fiber board and hard wood fiber board. Porous wood fiber boards are made up of loosely packed wood fibers, allowing for better insulation and sound absorption properties. On the other hand, hard wood fiber boards consist of tightly compressed wood fibers, making them durable and suitable for structural applications. Both types of wood fiber boards are commonly used in construction, furniture making, and various other industries for their unique features and benefits.</p></p>
