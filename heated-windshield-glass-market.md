<p><p>Heated windshield glass technology comes in two main types: heated wire windshields and heated coated windshields. Heated wire windshields have thin wires embedded in the glass that heat up to melt snow and ice. On the other hand, heated coated windshields have a thin layer of conductive material applied to the glass that also heats up to clear the windshield. Both types of heated windshield glass are designed to improve visibility and safety during cold weather conditions.</p></p>
