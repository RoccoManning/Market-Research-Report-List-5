<p><p>The automotive air purifier market encompasses various types of technologies, including HEPA filters, activated carbon filters, photocatalytic filters, and ionic filters. HEPA filters are known for their high efficiency in capturing small particles and allergens. Activated carbon filters effectively absorb odors and harmful gases. Photocatalytic filters use UV light to break down pollutants. Ionic filters release negatively charged ions to attract particles in the air. Each type of air purifier offers unique benefits in improving air quality inside vehicles.</p></p>
