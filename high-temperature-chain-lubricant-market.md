<p><p>High Temperature Chain Lubricant market includes two main types: Synthetic Oil and Semi-synthetic Oil. They offer higher lubricity and thermal stability, making them ideal for high temperature applications. Semi-synthetic oils, on the other hand, are a blend of synthetic and mineral oils. They offer a balance between performance and cost, providing adequate lubrication and protection at high temperatures while being more cost-effective than pure synthetic oils.</p></p>
