<p><p>A coating plate is a type of printing plate used in the printing industry to transfer ink onto a substrate. These plates typically consist of a thin layer of photosensitive material coated onto a metal base, such as aluminum or steel. <p><p>Coating plate market types consist of cold rolled plate, hot galvanized plate, electric galvanized plate, and other variants. Cold rolled plate undergoes a process where the steel is rolled at room temperature, resulting in a smooth and durable finish. Hot galvanized plate is coated with a layer of zinc through a hot-dipping process, providing superior corrosion resistance. Electric galvanized plate is similarly coated with zinc but uses an electric current instead. Other coating plate markets may include different surface finishes or alternative coating materials.</p></p>
