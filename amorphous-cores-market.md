<p><p>Amorphous cores are a type of soft magnetic material used in various applications such as transformers and inductors. Two common types of amorphous cores are toroidal cores and cut cores. Toroidal cores are ring-shaped and provide efficient magnetic flux paths, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Cut cores are made by cutting and stacking amorphous strips, offering flexibility in design and customization. Both types of amorphous cores are known for their low core losses and high magnetic flux density, making them popular choices in many industries.</p></p>
