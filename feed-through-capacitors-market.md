There are three main types of Feed Through Capacitors in the market: Ceramic Feed Through Capacitors, which offer high capacitance values and low leakage currents; Aluminum Feed Through Capacitors, known for their high voltage ratings and low impedance characteristics; and Film Feed Through Capacitors, which provide excellent stability and reliability.
