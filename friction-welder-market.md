<p><p>Friction welding is a process that joins materials through the application of heat generated by friction. The market is divided into three main types: rotary friction welder, which rotates one piece against another to create the heat needed for welding; linear friction welder, where one piece moves linearly against another to achieve the same result; and friction stir welder, which uses a rotating tool to join materials. Each type offers specific advantages and is suitable for different applications in various industries.</p></p>
