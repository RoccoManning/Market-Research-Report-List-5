<p><p>Electric Power Steering (EPS) Control Units market is categorized into various types based on different control mechanisms. The types include Column-EPS (C-EPS) which integrates the control unit with the steering column, Pinion-EPS (P-EPS) where the control unit is located on the steering gear, Rack-EPS (R-EPS) which has the control unit integrated into the steering rack, and Dual-Pinion EPS (DP-EPS) which utilizes two pinions for enhanced steering control. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of performance and efficiency.</p></p>
