<p><p>In the full charter market, an entire aircraft is dedicated to one customer's shipment, providing exclusive use of the aircraft. This option is ideal for large shipments or time-sensitive deliveries. In the split charter market, multiple customers share space on the same aircraft, reducing costs for each party. This option is suitable for smaller shipments or when individual customers do not require the entire aircraft capacity. Both options offer flexibility and efficiency in air freight forwarding services.</p></p>
