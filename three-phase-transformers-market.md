<p><ul><li>Core-Type Three-Phase Transformer(Single Core,Two Cores)</li><li>Shell-Type Three-Phase Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Core-type three-phase transformers are commonly used in power distribution due to their efficiency and compact design. They come in two variations: single core and two cores. Single core transformers have all three phases wound on a single magnetic core, while two core transformers have each phase wound on separate cores. Shell-type three-phase transformers are another popular option, where the windings are placed around a central core. They are known for their high efficiency and ability to handle high levels of power.</p></p>
