<p><strong>The MRI Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Permanent Magnet MRI</li><li>Superconductive MRI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Permanent Magnet MRI systems use a stationary magnetic field generated by a permanent magnet, offering lower operating costs and ease of installation. Superconductive MRI systems, on the other hand, use superconducting coils cooled by liquid helium, providing a stronger magnetic field for higher image quality. Superconductive MRI systems are often preferred in larger hospitals and imaging centers due to their superior image resolution and faster scan times, while Permanent Magnet MRI systems are more commonly used in smaller clinics and physician offices.</p></p>
