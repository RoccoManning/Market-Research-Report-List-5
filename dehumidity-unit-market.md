<p><p>Dehumidity units come in various types to cater to different needs. Cooling dehumidifiers use a cooling coil to remove moisture from the air. Rotary dehumidifiers use a rotating wheel with a desiccant material to absorb moisture. Pipeline dehumidifiers are connected to the HVAC system to dehumidify the entire space. Electroosmosis dehumidifiers use an electric field to remove moisture from the air. Each type of dehumidifier has its own unique features and benefits to suit different applications.</p></p>
