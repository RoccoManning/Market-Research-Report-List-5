<p><p>Diffractive beam splitters are used in various applications including beam shaping, beam splitting, and beam foci. Beam shaping or top-hat beam splitters are designed to create a uniform intensity distribution across a beam profile. Beam splitting types of beam splitters divide a single input beam into multiple output beams. Beam foci beam splitters concentrate light energy onto a specific focal point. Each of these market types caters to different needs and requirements in fields such as laser processing, microscopy, and optical communications.</p></p>
