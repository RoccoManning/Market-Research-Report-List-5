<p><p>Polyimide films are a type of engineering plastic known for their high thermal stability, electrical insulation properties, and chemical resistance. There are two main types of polyimide films in the market - Benzene Polyimide Films and Biphenyl Polyimide Films. Benzene polyimide films offer excellent mechanical strength and are commonly used in applications where thermal stability is crucial. Biphenyl polyimide films, on the other hand, provide enhanced flexibility and are preferred in applications where high-temperature resistance and dimensional stability are required.</p></p>
