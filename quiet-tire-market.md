<p><p>Quiet tires can be classified into two main market types based on their construction: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires have a more flexible sidewall and are constructed with steel belts running perpendicular to the direction of travel, providing better stability and fuel efficiency. Bias tires, on the other hand, have a stiffer sidewall and are constructed with diagonal ply layers, offering a smoother ride and better traction in off-road conditions. Both types have their own advantages and are popular choices in the tire market.</p></p>
