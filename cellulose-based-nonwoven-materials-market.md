<p><strong>The Cellulose-based Nonwoven Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spunlace Methods</li><li>Needle-punched Methods</li><li>Meltblowing Methods</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cellulose-based nonwoven materials market includes various types such as spunlace methods, needle-punched methods, and meltblowing methods. Spunlace methods involve high-pressure water jets to entangle fibers, creating a strong and durable fabric. Needle-punched methods use barbed needles to mechanically interlock fibers, resulting in a sturdy material suitable for applications requiring high tensile strength. Meltblowing methods melt cellulose fibers and then extrude them through tiny nozzles to form a fine fiber web. Each method offers unique characteristics and advantages for different end-uses.</p></p>
