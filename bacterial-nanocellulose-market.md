Two main types of bacterial nanocellulose products include nanofibrillated cellulose and nanocrystalline cellulose. Nanofibrillated cellulose is made up of cellulose fibers that are broken down into smaller nanofibers, while nanocrystalline cellulose consists of cellulose crystals with high strength and stiffness. Both types of bacterial nanocellulose have applications in fields such as biomedical, packaging, and textiles.</p></p>
