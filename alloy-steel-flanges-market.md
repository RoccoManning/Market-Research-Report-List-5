<p><ul><li>Blind Flange</li><li>Weld Neck Flange</li><li>Slip-On Flange</li><li>Socket Weld Flange</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Alloy steel flanges market includes various types such as Blind Flange, Weld Neck Flange, Slip-On Flange, Socket Weld Flange, and Others. Blind flanges are solid disks used to block off a pipeline or to create a stop. Weld neck flanges are designed to be joined to a piping system by butt welding. Slip-on flanges are attached by welding the pipe to the flange. Socket weld flanges are counter-bored to accept the pipe before being welded. Others include custom-made or specialty flanges not falling into the mentioned categories.</p></p>
