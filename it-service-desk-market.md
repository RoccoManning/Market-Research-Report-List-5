<p><p>IT Service Desk market can be classified into two main types: Cloud-based and On-Premise solutions. Cloud-based service desks are hosted on servers off-site and accessible over the internet, providing flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, On-Premise service desks are installed and managed within the organization's infrastructure, offering more control over security and customization. Both types serve the same purpose of providing IT support and resolving technical issues, but they vary in deployment, maintenance, and accessibility.</p></p>
