<p><p>Large Display Driver ICs can be categorized into two main types: Source Display Driver IC and Gate Display Driver IC. Source Display Driver ICs are responsible for controlling the current to each pixel in a display, while Gate Display Driver ICs manage the activation of each row of pixels. Source drivers are typically used in high-resolution displays, while Gate drivers are commonly found in larger displays such as TVs and monitors. Both types of drivers play a crucial role in ensuring the proper functioning of large displays.</p></p>
