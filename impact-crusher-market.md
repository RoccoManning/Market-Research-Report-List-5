<p><p>Impact crushers are widely used in crushing applications due to their high efficiency and versatility. Two main types are horizontal and vertical impact crushers in the market. Horizontal impact crushers are designed for primary and secondary crushing applications, while vertical impact crushers are ideal for tertiary and quaternary crushing. Horizontal crushers offer better reduction ratios and can handle larger feed sizes, whereas vertical crushers are better suited for producing cubical end products. Both types cater to different crushing needs and are essential in various industries.</p></p>
