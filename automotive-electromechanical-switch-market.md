<p><p>The Automotive Electromechanical Switch market offers various types to cater to different needs. Tactile switches provide a tactile feedback when pressed. Rotary switches involve rotation to make electrical connections. Encoder switches convert shaft rotation into digital signals. Toggle switches have a lever for on/off operation. Push switches are pressed to make a connection. Detect switches sense motion or presence of objects. Micro switches are compact and sensitive. Dip switches are used for setting electronic configurations. Other types include Power switches, Slide switches, and Joy Stick switches.</p></p>
