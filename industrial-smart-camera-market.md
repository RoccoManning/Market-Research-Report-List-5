<p><strong>The Industrial Smart Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area Scan Camera</li><li>Line Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial smart cameras can be categorized into two main types: area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras take a snapshot of the entire scene at once, making them suitable for applications such as inspection, quality control, and robotics. Line scan cameras capture an image one line at a time, making them ideal for continuous inspection of moving objects, such as in production lines or conveyor belts. Both types of cameras are essential for industrial processes where precision and efficiency are paramount.</p></p>
