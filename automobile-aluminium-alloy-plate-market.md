<p><p>Automobile Aluminium Alloy Plate market is segmented into two types: Cold Rolling and Hot Rolling. Cold rolling involves passing the aluminum alloy plate through a pair of rolls at room temperature to achieve a smooth and uniform surface finish. Hot rolling, on the other hand, involves heating the aluminum alloy plate above its recrystallization temperature and then rolling it to achieve desired thickness and surface characteristics. Both methods have their own advantages and are used in the automobile industry based on specific requirements.</p></p>
