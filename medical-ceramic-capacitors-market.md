<p><p>Medical ceramic capacitors are used in both implantable and non-implantable medical devices. Non-implantable ceramic capacitors are used in external medical devices, such as monitors or diagnostic equipment, where they help to store and release electrical energy. Both types of capacitors play a crucial role in ensuring the proper functioning of medical equipment and ultimately the health and safety of patients.</p></p>
