<p><strong>The Aromatic Extract Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Chlorination (DC) process</li><li>Hydrochlorinaton (HC) process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aromatic extracts are produced through two main processes in the market:</p><p>1. Direct Chlorination (DC) process involves passing chlorine gas through a heated mixture of aromatic compounds to produce aromatic extracts. This process is efficient and cost-effective, making it popular in the market.</p><p>2. Hydrochlorination (HC) process involves the reaction of hydrogen chloride with aromatic compounds to produce aromatic extracts. This process is more selective and produces higher quality extracts compared to the DC process. However, it can be more energy-intensive and expensive.</p></p>
