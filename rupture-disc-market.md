<p><ul><li>Positive Arch Rupture Disc</li><li>Anti-arch Rupture Disc</li><li>Flat Type Rupture Disc</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rupture discs are categorized into different types based on their design and operating characteristics. Positive Arch rupture discs have an arch-shaped design that allows for accurate burst pressure control. Anti-arch rupture discs are designed to prevent reverse buckling and offer high burst pressure capabilities. Flat Type rupture discs have a flat surface that ruptures when pressure exceeds a certain threshold. Other types of rupture discs include composite discs, reverse-acting discs, and pre-scored discs, each offering unique features suited for specific applications in industries such as oil and gas, chemical processing, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
