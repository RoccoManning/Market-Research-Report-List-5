<p><p>Cold forming machines are categorized based on the number of dies they have, each serving a specific purpose in the production process. 3-die station machines are versatile and can create complex shapes. 1-die stations are simple and used for basic forming tasks. 2-die stations offer increased efficiency by performing two operations simultaneously. 4-die stations are capable of high-volume production. 5-die stations further enhance production speed and precision. Other machines in the market may include custom or specialty machines designed for specific manufacturing needs.</p></p>
