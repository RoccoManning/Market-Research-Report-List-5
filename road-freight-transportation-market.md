<p><strong>The Road Freight Transportation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full Truckload</li><li>Less-Than-Truckload</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Road Freight Transportation Market comprises of two main types: Full Truckload (FTL) and Less-Than-Truckload (LTL) market. FTL involves the transportation of goods that fill an entire truck, usually for a single customer. It is more cost-effective for shippers with large shipments. On the other hand, LTL is for smaller shipments that do not require a full truck. Multiple shippers share the space on one truck, making it a more economical option for smaller businesses. Both FTL and LTL play a crucial role in meeting the diverse shipping needs of businesses.</p></p>
