<p><p>Plasma surface treatment equipment can be categorized into two main types: low pressure/vacuum plasma surface treatment equipment and atmospheric plasma surface treatment equipment. Low pressure/vacuum plasma equipment operates in a controlled environment with reduced pressure, making it suitable for delicate materials. On the other hand, atmospheric plasma equipment functions at normal pressure levels, offering a cost-effective solution for high-volume production processes. Both types of equipment are utilized for enhancing surface properties through processes such as cleaning, activation, and coating.</p></p>
