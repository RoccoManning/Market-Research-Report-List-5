<p><strong>The Optical Fiber Lighting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>End-emitting Fiber</li><li>Edge-emitting Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical fiber lighting is classified into two main types: end-emitting fiber and edge-emitting fiber. End-emitting fiber sends light through the end of the fiber, making it ideal for applications like signage and point light sources. On the other hand, edge-emitting fiber emits light from the sides, making it suitable for evenly illuminating large areas such as ceilings and walls. Both types offer unique benefits and are commonly used in various lighting applications due to their flexibility and durability.</p></p>
