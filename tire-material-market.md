<p><ul><li>Elastomers</li><li>Reinforcing Fillers</li><li>Plasticizers</li><li>Chemicals</li><li>Metal Reinforcements</li><li>Textile Reinforcements</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tire material market consists of various types of materials that are used in the manufacturing of tires. Elastomers are the base material that provides flexibility and durability to the tire. Reinforcing fillers such as carbon black or silica enhance the strength and wear resistance of the tire. Plasticizers are additives that improve the flexibility of the tire material. Chemicals are used for various purposes such as bonding agents or antioxidants. Metal reinforcements like steel belts provide added strength to the tire structure. Textile reinforcements like polyester or nylon cords provide additional strength and flexibility to the tire.</p></p>
