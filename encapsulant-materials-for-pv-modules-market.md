<p><strong>The Encapsulant Materials for PV Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EVA Film</li><li>POE Film</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Encapsulant materials for PV modules are primarily classified into three types: EVA film, POE film, and others. EVA film, or ethylene-vinyl acetate, is a popular choice for its excellent light transmittance and flexibility. POE film, or polyolefin elastomer, is known for its superior durability and weather resistance. Other encapsulant materials include TPU film, silicone gel, and PVB film. These materials are crucial for protecting the solar cells within the module from external environmental factors, ensuring the longevity and efficiency of the PV system.</p></p>
