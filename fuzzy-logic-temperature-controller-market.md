<p><ul><li>Open-loop Controller</li><li>Closed-loop Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fuzzy logic temperature controllers are used in two main market types: Open-loop controllers and Closed-loop controllers. Open-loop controllers make decisions based solely on input, without any feedback from the system. They are generally simpler and less expensive but less accurate. Closed-loop controllers, on the other hand, use feedback from the system to make decisions, resulting in more precise temperature control. They are typically more complex and costlier but offer better performance in maintaining desired temperatures.</p></p>
