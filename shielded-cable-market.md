<p><p>A Shielded Cable is a type of electrical cable which contains insulating materials such as a layer of foil or braided material around the inner conductor to minimize electromagnetic interference (EMI) and radio frequency interference (RFI). This shielding helps to protect the cable from external electrical noise and ensures the signal quality remains intact. Shielded cables are commonly used in industries where high levels of EMI and RFI are present, such as telecommunications, aerospace, military, and industrial applications.</p><p>The Shielded Cable Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.80% during the forecast period. <p><p>Shielded cable market types include metal foil shield, compile net shield, and others. Metal foil shield cables have a layer of thin metal foil surrounding the inner components to protect against electromagnetic interference. Compile net shield cables use a woven metal mesh around the inner components for heightened protection. Other types of shielded cables may include combination shields or unique designs tailored to specific industry needs. Each type of shielded cable offers different levels of protection against interference to ensure optimal performance in varying environments.</p></p>
