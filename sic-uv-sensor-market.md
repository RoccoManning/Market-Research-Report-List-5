<p><p>The SiC UV Sensor market includes three main types: UVA Sensor, UVB Sensor, and UVC Sensor. UVA Sensors are designed to detect ultraviolet light in the range of 320 to 400 nm, while UVB Sensors detect light in the range of 280 to 320 nm. UVC Sensors, on the other hand, are used to detect the shortest wavelength UV light, ranging from 200 to 280 nm. Each type of sensor is specialized for detecting specific wavelengths of ultraviolet light and has unique applications in diverse industries.</p></p>
