<p><strong>The Square Port Knife Gate Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metal Seat</li><li>Resilient Seat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal seat knife gate valves are designed for applications where high temperatures, corrosive fluids, or abrasive materials are present. These valves have a metal-to-metal seal that provides tight shutoff even in harsh conditions. Resilient seat knife gate valves, on the other hand, are equipped with a soft elastomer seat that provides bubble-tight shutoff and is suitable for applications with less severe conditions. Both types of valves are commonly used in industries such as mining, pulp and paper, wastewater treatment, and power generation.</p></p>
