<p><p>The Bicycle Suspension System market comprises two main types: Hardtail Suspension and Full Suspension. Hardtail Suspension bikes have a suspension fork in the front but no suspension in the rear, providing a simpler and lighter design suited for smoother terrain. On the other hand, Full Suspension bikes feature both front and rear suspension systems, offering better control and comfort on rough and uneven surfaces. These two types cater to different rider preferences and terrain conditions, providing options for a wide range of cycling enthusiasts.</p></p>
