<p><p>Ceramic foams are categorized into two main types based on their structure: open-hole ceramic material and closed-hole ceramic material. Open-hole ceramic material has interconnected voids throughout the structure, allowing for greater permeability and fluid flow. On the other hand, closed-hole ceramic material has isolated voids within the material, providing better insulation properties. Both types of ceramic foams are used in various applications such as thermal insulation, filtration, and structural components in industries like aerospace, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
