<p><p>The exhaust fans market includes various types such as centrifugal exhaust fans, inline centrifugal exhaust fans, radial exhaust fans, tubeaxial, and vaneaxial exhaust fans. Centrifugal exhaust fans use rotating impellers to generate airflow. Inline centrifugal exhaust fans are mounted within ductwork. Radial exhaust fans have straight blades to move air radially. Tubeaxial exhaust fans have axial flow in a cylindrical housing. Vaneaxial exhaust fans have vanes to direct airflow. Each type caters to specific ventilation needs in various industrial and commercial settings.</p></p>
