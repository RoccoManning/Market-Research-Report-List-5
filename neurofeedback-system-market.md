<p><p>Neurofeedback systems have different types based on the technology and techniques used. Slow Cortical Potential Neurofeedback (SCP-NF) focuses on training brain wave activity to improve attention and self-regulation. Low-energy Neurofeedback System (LENS) uses very low-frequency electromagnetic fields to enhance brain function. Hemoencephalographic (HEG) neurofeedback measures blood flow in the brain to enhance cognitive function. Other markets include techniques like Alpha-Theta Neurofeedback and Z-Score Neurofeedback, which target specific brain functions for various therapeutic purposes. Each type of neurofeedback system offers unique benefits for improving brain health and cognitive performance.</p></p>
