<p><p>Semiconductor chip packaging market types include Fan-Out Wafer-Level Packaging (FO WLP), Fan-In Wafer-Level Packaging (FI WLP), Flip Chip (FC), and 2.5D/3D packaging. FO WLP and FI WLP involve packaging chips directly on the wafer, with FO WLP allowing for more input/output connections. Flip Chip packaging involves flipping the chip to connect it to the substrate, allowing for higher performance and density. 2.5D/3D packaging involves stacking multiple chips on top of each other to increase processing power and reduce footprint.</p></p>
