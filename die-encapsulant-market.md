<p><p>Die encapsulant market is divided into two main types, epoxy-based and silicone-based. Epoxy-based encapsulants offer excellent adhesion and thermal conductivity, making them ideal for high-power devices. Meanwhile, silicone-based encapsulants are known for their flexibility and resistance to moisture and chemicals, making them suitable for sensitive electronic components. Both types of encapsulants provide protection for semiconductor devices from environmental factors, ensuring their durability and reliability in various applications.</p></p>
