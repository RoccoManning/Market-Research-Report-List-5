<p><p>Fixed firefighting misting systems are divided into two main types: high pressure and non-high pressure systems. High pressure systems utilize pressurized water to create a fine mist that effectively suppresses fires. These systems are typically used in industrial settings or areas with high fire risk. On the other hand, non-high pressure systems operate without pressurized water and are often used in residential or smaller commercial buildings. Both types of systems are vital for ensuring quick and effective fire suppression.</p></p>
