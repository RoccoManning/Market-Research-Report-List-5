<p><p>Stopper bolts come in two main market types: Half Bolt and Full Bolt. Half bolts are designed to penetrate halfway into the material, providing a strong anchor point while allowing for easy removal if necessary. Full bolts, on the other hand, penetrate fully into the material, providing maximum stability and security. Full bolts are typically used in applications where a secure and permanent attachment is required, while half bolts are used in situations where flexibility in removal is needed. Both types offer different levels of strength and versatility to meet various construction and installation needs.</p></p>
