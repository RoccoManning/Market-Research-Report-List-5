<p><ul><li>Two-terminal HVDC System</li><li>Multi-terminal HVDC System (MTDC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HVDC systems are used to transmit high voltage direct current over long distances with minimal power loss. The two main types of HVDC systems are Two-terminal HVDC System and Multi-terminal HVDC System (MTDC). Two-terminal systems have two converter stations connected by a single transmission line, while MTDC systems have multiple converter stations connected in a network configuration. MTDC systems are more complex and offer greater flexibility and reliability, making them suitable for transmitting power in interconnected grids and offshore wind farms.</p></p>
