<p><p>Multi-layer flexible packaging market can be categorized into two main types based on the loading method: top loading and bottom loading. In top loading packaging, the product is inserted into the package from the top opening, making it suitable for products like snacks or grains. On the other hand, bottom loading packaging involves inserting the product from the bottom, making it ideal for liquids or powders. Both types of packaging offer convenience and versatility in terms of packaging different types of products efficiently.</p></p>
