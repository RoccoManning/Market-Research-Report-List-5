<p><p>Infrared filters are used in various applications such as photography, security systems, and scientific research to block unwanted light while allowing infrared light to pass through. There are two main types of infrared filters - Glass Type and Film Type. Glass Type filters are made of special optical glass that allows infrared light to pass through, while Film Type filters are made of thin film coatings applied to glass or plastic substrates. Both types of filters play a crucial role in enhancing the quality of infrared imaging and sensing systems.</p></p>
