<p><p>Vessel-Based Automatic Identification Services (AIS) involve the use of AIS transponders installed on ships to transmit location and other relevant data to other vessels and onshore stations. This information helps improve navigation and avoid collisions at sea. Onshore-Based AIS, on the other hand, involves the use of fixed AIS receivers placed onshore to monitor vessel movements in a specific region or waterway. This data can be used for port management, maritime security, and environmental monitoring purposes.</p></p>
