<p><p>Silicone leather can be categorized into two main types: silicone resin leather and silicone rubber leather. Silicone resin leather is made by combining a silicone resin with natural leather, creating a material that is durable, water-resistant, and heat-resistant. On the other hand, silicone rubber leather is made by applying a layer of silicone rubber onto a fabric base, resulting in a material that is flexible, soft, and waterproof. Both types of silicone leather offer unique properties that make them popular in various industries such as fashion, automotive, and upholstery.</p></p>
