<p><p>Reusable Launch Systems can be divided into two main types: Horizontal Take-off, Horizontal Landing (HTHL) systems. In a HTHL system, the launch vehicle takes off vertically like a traditional rocket but lands horizontally like an airplane. This allows for increased flexibility and reusability, as the system can land on conventional runways instead of requiring specialized landing pads. These systems are well-suited for short-duration missions and are gaining popularity in the aerospace industry due to their cost-effectiveness and efficiency.</p></p>
