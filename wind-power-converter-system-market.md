<p><strong>The Wind Power Converter System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Doubly-Fed</li><li>Full Power</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wind Power Converter System market can be categorized into two types: Doubly-Fed and Full Power. Doubly-Fed converters use a rotor winding connected to the AC grid through a partial power electronic converter, allowing for variable speed operation and improved efficiency. On the other hand, Full Power converters have the entire power flow passing through the power electronics, enabling better control of the turbine and full power operation. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and reliability of wind power systems.</p></p>
