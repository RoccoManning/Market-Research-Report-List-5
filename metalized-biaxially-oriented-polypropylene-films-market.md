<p><p>Metalized Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene Films come in two main types: Heat Sealable and Non Heat Sealable. Heat Sealable films have a coating that allows them to be sealed using heat, making them ideal for packaging that needs to be sealed securely. Non Heat Sealable films do not have this sealing capability and are often used for applications where heat sealing is not necessary, such as decorative or reflective purposes. Both types offer excellent barrier properties and are widely used in packaging, labeling, and lamination applications.</p></p>
