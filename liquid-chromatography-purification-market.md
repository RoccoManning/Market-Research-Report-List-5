<p><p>Liquid chromatography purification market has two main types: single-step purification and multiple-step purification. In single-step purification, the process involves the separation of target compounds from impurities in a single chromatographic step, leading to a quicker and more efficient purification process. On the other hand, multiple-step purification involves multiple rounds of chromatographic separation to achieve a higher level of purity, making it ideal for complex mixtures or when a high degree of purity is required. Both types cater to different needs and requirements in the field of purification.</p></p>
